Introduction
 Dungen is a rogue-like RPG that is written in Javascript and playable in the browser. In the game, the player controls one or more characters as they adventure down into the depths of the dungeon, defeating monsters, increasing their skills and collecting a wealth of equipment and treasure.

Characters
 The player initially gets to choose a character to play as, and as the game progresses, more characters will join the team. Each character has a class which defines what equipment their can use as well as their starting attributes. Once the characters gain enough experience, they level-up and the player can choose how the character's attributes develop. The attributes are:
- Strength, which affects how much damage weapons do.
- Agility, which affects how efficiently characters use weapons and can allow characters to dodge blows.
- Endurance, affects the character's total HP and EP
- Wisdom, effectiveness of magical spells that the character casts - including attacks with staffs.
- Will, affects the character's total EP increases and also defines their resistance to magical attacks.

Knight
The Knight uses a sword to attack and is equipped with a shield, armour and a helmet for defense. The knight is not restricted in what defensiveness equipment he can use, which means his defense is high, suitable to close range combat with the sword. This combat style is matched by his high strength and endurance, as well as reasonable agility.

Mage
The Mage is the opposite of the knight. His wisdom is high to enable him to perform effective ranged magical attacks with his staff as well as resisting incoming spells as well. The mage has low strength and can only equip a limited set of defensive clothing, so he has to rely on being agile enough to dodge blows as well as using defensive and recovery spells.

Rogue
The Rogue uses a balance of strength and agility to land deadly blows while dodging attacks. He can equip a good selection of armours, except for the heavier ones which would impede his movements. The rogue uses blades to attack close range and also can use a selection of throwing blades to attack from medium range.

Archer
The Archer is a long range combantant who uses a combination of bows and arrows to deal high damage from afar. The Archer's key skill is agility to enable him to fire arrows in quick succession, but he also needs strength to draw his bow. Like the Rogue, he can equip a good selection of armour and helmets but not the heavier items.

Warlock
The Warlock is a physically strong character who wields an axe but can also use spells. The warlock has strong will, endurance and strength but is not very agile.



Game Play
 Turn-based action
 Dungen is a turn-based game which allows each character (including enemies) to take their turn in order. During a character's turn, it can perform an action as long as it has enough energy points (EP), if it doesn't have enough EP then the character rests for that turn and recovers some EP. Movements and attacks both use EP with attacks using heavier weapons and/or by a less agile character will consume more EP. Each character has health points (HP) too and when this value hits zero, the character collapses and will not take another turn until they have been revived. When a monster dies, the heroes in the team gain experience points (XP) which when accumulated allow the characters to level-up which increases maximum HP, EP and their attributes.


Equipment and Items
 Each character has four equipment slots: their primary attack weapon, their secondary item, body armour and a helmet. The class of the characters defines which item types they can and can't use. As the game progresses the player will encounter chests placed throughout the dungeons which contain equipment and potions and treasure that will help the team survive their adventure.

Controls
Control of the heroes is performed purely through touch or mouse clicks. The player will click or tap on targets or items they want to interact with as well as some on-screen buttons that are provided for menus, etc... In the bottom right of the screen is the main controller button, which looks like a directional controller from an old gamepad. On clicking this button, another set of buttons will appear above it which enable the player to access menus and take team actions.
- Centre camera on the hero.
- Open or close equipment menu.
- Select single or team control
- Rest, will rest the party until they're fully rested or until another command is given.
- Heal, with quick button will select the weakest potion that the player is in possession of to heal the currently controlled hero.

Menus
 Clicking on the person button will bring up the menu for hero in the top right corner of the screen. The view will show the stats for the hero, with their current equipment displayed at the top. Clicking on any of the currently equipped items will bring up a list of alternative items that the player has in their inventory. Clicking on an item in this list will equip it to the hero.
