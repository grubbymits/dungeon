The Knight

The Knight is the most sturdy warrior, ready to slash and bash his way through the endless dungeons. He is equipped with a sword, can block with a shield, and wear all helmets and armour.

Select Knight
The Mage

The Mage is the most wise dungeon explorer, capable of using magical staff to cast spells on friends and foes from afar. The Wizard is however limited in what he can wear in armour, but his high mental strength protects him well from magical attacks.

Select Mage
The Rogue

The Rogue is the most nimble of the dungeon crawlers, his attacks are fast and he's frequently able to dodge others attacks. What the rogue lacks in defense, he makes up for being able to perform range attacks as well as melee.

Select Rogue
The Archer

The Archer is the ultimate in distance combat, being able to use a combination of bows and arrows. He is also agile and can wear an assortment of helmets and armours.

Select Archer
